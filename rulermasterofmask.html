Ruler Master of the Mask

A drama that filled with love, hatred and jealousy

Profile

Title of Drama: Ruler Master of the Mask
Director: No Do Cheol and Park Won-Gook
Writer: Park Hye-Jin, Jung Hae-Ri
Episode: 40
Release Date: May 10-July 13 2019
Runtime: Wednesday & Thursday 22.00
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Yoo Seung Ho as Lee Sun
Kim So Hyun as Han Ga-Eun
Kim Myung Soo as Lee Sun
Yoon So-Hee as Kim HwaGoon
Heo Jun-Ho as Dae Mok

Synopsis

n the 1700s, the Crown Prince of Joseon (Yoo Seung Ho) fights against Pyunsoo Hwe. It is a organisation that has a lot of power and nobdoy dares to disobey any of their rules. This organisation even control the water of the country. Ga Eun is the woman that Crown Princes love at first sight. Ga Eun also help him to grow as a ruler.

Feelings about this drama

This is one of the drama that I would not mind watching it again! The plot of this drama was extremely well writen and the casts were able to act out the role so well, thanks to their charisma. However, there were many sad scenes in this drama so prepare a box of tissue before watching this drama. I would definitely recommend this drama to anyone who love to watch historical and romance drama!
