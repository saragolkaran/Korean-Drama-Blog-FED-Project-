Touch Your Heart

A drama that filled with love, hatred and jealousy

Profile

Title of Drama: Touch Your Heart
Director: Park Joon-Hwa
Writer: Lee Myung-suk, Choi Bo-rim
Episode: 16
Release Date: February 6 to March 28, 2019
Runtime: Wendesday and Thursday, 21.30
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Lee Dong Wook as Kwon Jung-Rok
Yoo In-Na as Oh Yoon-Seo / Oh Jin-Sim
Lee Sang-Woo as Kim Se-Won
Son Sung-Yoon as Nam Gyoo Man
Son Sung-Yoon as Yeon Joon-Kyu

Synopsis

Oh Jin Shim is a top actress. She uses a stage name called Oh Yoon Seo. She has a scandal and because of this, she lost her job which cause her to be jobless for two years.

She wanted to get a role in a major upcoming television series. In order for her to do that, she eventually agreed to work at a law firm as a secretary for a lawyer called Kwon Jung Rok.

Feelings about this drama

This is a very cute drama. The pkot of this drama was well written and the casts were able bring out their role so well, this is all because of their many years of experience and their charisma! I strongly recommend this drama to those who love to watch romantic comedy drama!
