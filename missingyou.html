Missing You

A drama that filled with love, revenge, hatred and jealousy

Profile

Title of Drama: Missing You
Hangul: 보고싶다
Director: Lee Jae Dong and Park Jae Bum
Writer: Moon Hee Jung
Episode: 21
Release Date: November 7, 2012 to January 17, 2013
Runtime: Wednesday and Thursday 21.55
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Park Yoo-Chun as Han Jung Woo
Yoon Eun-Hye as Lee Soo-Yeon / Joy
Yoo Seung Ho as Kang Hyung Joon / Harry
Jang Mi In Nae as Kim Eun Joo

Synopsis

15 year old Soo Yeon (Kim So Hyun) is a victim of bullying in middle school. Her classmates targeted her because her father is a murderer. She has an ally Jung Woo (Yeo Jin Goo), who always protected her from the bullies. Both of them fall in love with each other. Unfortunately, due to an unexpected accident, they became separated. 14 years later, they meet again through destiny.

Jung Woo (Park Yoo Chun) is now a detective and his first love has always remind him in the back of his mind. Soo Yeon (Yoon Eun Hye) is now a rookie fashion desginer who always tries her best to have a bright personality. Hyung Joon (Yoo Seung Ho) is Soo Yeon's boyfriend, who always appear warm and nice.

Feelings about this drama

This is the only drama so far that i would not mine rewatching again and again. I love the whole plot of this drama but I need to admit that this is one of the most depressing drama. There were many sad scenes in this drama but the casts were so talented that they are able to act out their role so well. I would definitely recommend this drama to everyone! This is one of the best drama ever!
