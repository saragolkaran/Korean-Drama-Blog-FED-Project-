Beautiful World

A drama that filled with love and comedy

Profile

Title of Drama: Clean With Passion For Now
Director: No Jong Chan
Writer: Han Hee Jung
Episode: 16
Release Date: November 26, 2018 to February 4, 2019
Runtime: Monday and Tuesday 21.00
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Yoon Gyun Sang as Jang Sun Gyeol
Kim You Jung as Gil O Sol
Song Jae Rim as Choi
Kim Hye Eun as Cha Mae Hwa
Ahn Suk Hwan as Ceo Cha

Synopsis

Jang Sun Gyeol has a fear of germs and he runs a cleaning company. He then meets Gil O Sol. She has a bright personality and she does not mind getting dirty. With the help of Gil O Sol, he faces his fear of germs and try very hard to overcome it. He then slowly falls in love with Gil O Sol.

Feelings about this drama

I love this drama so much and this is one of my best drama that i have ever watch! There are faith, hope and courage that I can take away from this drama! I am very satisfied with how this drama turned out. Watching this drama can really brighten up my day as mostly every episodes gave meaningful messages in each episode. All the episodes were filled with fun and interesting scenes and is very nice to see how the story is developing.
