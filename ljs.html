Lee Jong Suk

Profile

Name: Lee Jong Suk

Born: September 14. 1989
Height: 186cm

Filmography

Year Film
2013 I Can Hear Your Voice
2017 While You Were Sleeping

Lee Jong Suk was born on September 14, 1989. He is a South Korean Actor and a model. He debuted in 2005 as a runway model. He became the youngest male model ever to participate in Seoul Fashion Week. His breakout role was in School 2013. He is also well known for his roles in I Can Hear Your Voice in 2013, Doctor Stranger in 2014, Pinocchio in 2014, W in 2016, While You Were Sleeping in 2017, The Hymn of Death in 2018 and Romance Is a Bonus Book in 2019.
