Nam Da Reum

Profile

Name: Nam Da Reum
Hangul: 남다름
Born: June 13, 2002
Height: 186cm

Filmography

Year Film
2016 Memory
2017 Bad Thief, Good Thief
2018 Come and Hug Me

Nam Da Reum was born on June 13, 2002. He is a south Korean actor who began his career as a child actor. He played a young version of many leading characters of a variety of popular dramas such as Boys Over Flowers in 2009, Pinocchio in 2014, Rain or Shine in 2017, The King in Love in 2017, While You Were Sleeping in 2017 and Come and Hug Me in 2018.
