Welcome to Waikiki

A drama that filled with love, hatred and jealousy

Profile

Title of Drama: Welcome to Waikiki
Director: Lee Chang-Min
Writer: Kim Ki-Ho, Song Ji-Eun, Song Mi-So
Episode: 20
Release Date: February 5 - April 17, 2018
Runtime: Monday & Tuesday 23.00
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Kim Jung-Hyun as Kang Dong Goo
Lee Yi Kyung as Lee Joon Ki
Son Seung Won as Bong Doo Sik
Jung In Sun as Han Yoon A
Go Won Hee as Kanf Seo JIn

Synopsis

Kang Dong Goo (Kim Jung Hyun) dreams of becoming a movie director, but he is cynical due to bad luck. Cheon Joo Ki (Lee Yi Kyung) wanted to follow his father's pathway and become an actor. Unfortunately, he is now just a minor actor. Bong Doo Sik (Son Seung Won) came to seoul to become a writer, but things have not been easy for him at all.

These three men run the guest house Waikiki in Itaewon, South Korea. Their guest house was facing bankruptcy. A mysterious baby and single mother appear at waikiki out of the blue.

This drama is the another drama that I would notmind watching it again. The plot of this drama was so well written. After watching the first episode, I really have the urge to watch the next episode as soon as possible. The casts were able to potray their characters so well. There were many sweet and exciting scenes as well. I highly recommend ths drama to anyone who love to watch romantic comedy drama!
