Her Private Life

A drama that filled with love, passion and comedy

Profile

Title of Drama: Her Private Life
Director: Park Chan Hong
Writer: Kim Ji
Episode: 16
Release Date: April 5, 2019
Runtime: Fridays & Saturday 23.00
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Park Min-Young as Sung Deok-Mi
Kim Jae-Wook as Ryan
Ahn Bo-Hyun as Nam Eun-Ki
One as Cha Shi-An
Kim Bo-Ra as Cindy

Synopsis

Sung Deok-Mi (Park Min-Young) works as a curator at an art gallery. She is a huge fan of idol group member Shi-An and secretly runs a fan website about him. Ryan Gold (Kim Jae Wook) is a standoffish artist who suffer from Stendhal and due to this, he retires from being an artist. Uhm So Hye (Kim Sun Young) gets suspected from embezzlement thus he was being investigated by the police. Due to this reason, Ryan Gold became the new Art Director of an art gallery.

There are some rumors saying that Deok Mi is dating Shi An, because of this, Ryan suggest that he and Deok Mi should pretend to date.

Feelings about this drama

This drama has become my favourite romantic-comedy drama! There are so many things that i loved, but what made the drama nice is the mian leads. Not only they are perfect for each other, their chemistry with each other was the best. The thing that I love about their relationship is they are always so honest with each other and they were so cute! I would definitely recommend this drama to those who love to watch romanic-comedy genre.
