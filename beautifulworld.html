Beautiful World

A drama that filled with love, hatred and jealousy

Profile

Title of Drama: Beautiful World
Director: Park Chan Hong
Writer: Kim Ji
Episode: 16
Release Date: April 5, to May 25 2019
Runtime: Fridays & Saturday 23.00
Languauge: Korean

Casts

Choo Ja Hyun as Kang In Ha
Park Hee-Soon as Park Moo Jin
Nam Da-Reum as Park Sun-Ho
Kim Hwan-Hee as Park Soo-Ho
Lee Chung Ah as Kang Joon Ha
Cho Yeo Jeong as Seo Eun Joo
Oh Man Seok as Oh Jin Pyo

Synopsis

Park Moo Jin and Kang In Ha are a married couple. Park Moo Jin is working as a teacher in a high school and Kang In Ha runs a bakery. Both of them enjoy their lives with their two children. Their peaceful days eventually turn out into a extremely bad state. Their son, who is in the third grade in middle school, is seriously ingured due to the school violence. Due to this, Park Moo Jin and Kang In Ha try their best to reveal the truth.

Oh Jin Pyo and Seo Eun Joo are a married couple as well and they are both from wealthy families. Oh Jin Pyo is the chairman of a private school foundation inheirted from his family. Both of them have never experienced failure or lacked anything. They have a son who get into trouble and their parents try their best to coverup for their son's own mistake as he did not want to admit it and tried to blame it on his other friends.

Feelings about this drama

This is one of the best drama that i have ever watched! It teach all of us a heavy lesson on bullying and we need to pay a heavy price for bullying other people, evn our friends. There were a mixed of emotions in this drama thus making it so addicting to watch all the episodes! This drama is trying to highlight the issue of bullying to everyone out there. This drama also taught us to always keep our hopes high and stay positive as well!
